The GIANTS Lab

Geometry-Informed Advances in Nano-Tunable Solids

What GIANTS Means

GIANTS stands for Geometry-Informed Advances in Nano-Tunable Solids. It is a research framework centered on the idea that the geometry of framework solids can be decoded, programmed, and actuated to unlock emergent properties and functional responses.

Rather than treating structure as a static background, GIANTS views geometry as an active design variable that governs synthesis, mechanics, adsorption, transport, and stimulus-responsive behavior in reticular solids.

Core Framework

The GIANTS concept is organized around three mutually connected actions: Decode, Design, and Actuate. These three directions form a closed research loop for understanding and engineering nano-tunable solids.

Decode focuses on identifying hidden geometrical factors that determine materials behavior. Design uses those insights to guide the synthesis and construction of framework solids. Actuate explores how external stimuli can trigger controlled structural and functional responses.

Research Vision

Through this framework, the GIANTS Lab aims to establish a geometry-based language for reticular chemistry, connecting structural motifs to dynamic properties and enabling the rational creation of advanced porous materials.

Keywords: reticular chemistry, framework solids, geometrical factors, molecular separation, dynamic porous materials, mechanically responsive materials
