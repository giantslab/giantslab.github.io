GIANTS

Geometry-Informed Advances in Nano-Tunable Solids

This page introduces the core research theme of GIANTS.

Overview

GIANTS represents a geometry-informed strategy for understanding and designing nano-tunable solids, with a particular focus on structure–property relationships in reticular materials.

Keywords

Reticular chemistry, porous materials, framework geometry, gas adsorption, molecular separation, and mechanically responsive solids.
